Behnoosh Zamanlooy

I develop trustworthy AI modules by analyzing the foundational constraints that ethical requirements impose on machine learning and statistical tasks involving sensitive data. Leveraging these insights, I design machine learning architectures and mechanisms that are optimally private, fair, and interpretable.

  • Learn more

Privacy

I construct minimax-optimal locally differentially-private mechanisms using tools from information theory and by studying the mixing times of specific Markov chains.

Fairness

I develop fair AI to ensure equitability of deep learning pipelines trained with societally-biased data.

Algorithmic Game Theory

I develop deep learning powered game theoretic algorithms, which have been deployed by Cognomos.

AI Theory

In order to develop my ethical AI models, I routinely need to investigate, understand, and build on the theoretical foundations of deep learning models.
